Update Account Information

To change your phone number or other information associated with your account, go to the "Profile" section in our app.Please be reminded that the mobile number in your account can only be linked to one unique email address. If you wish to link it with another email address, you will need to do it on web.

Forgot my password

To reset your password, follow these steps: Go to the login screen Click on 'Forgot?' Enter your email address You will receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password. Be careful not to share your password with anyone. Our Customer Care team will never ask you for your password.

Unsubscribe to newsletter

To unsubscribe from our newsletters and other communications, use the unsubscribe link in the email. Note that you can't unsubscribe from order information, invoices, and other emails directly connected to your orders.

Account security and protectin

While we do the best we can from our end to keep your data secure, here's some steps you can take to have a safe order experience: 1. Never share your credentials and bank account details with anyone. riders, and restaurant/ store partners will never ask for sensitive information. This includes passwords, OTP, credit or debit card PIN, and card details such as card number, CVV, and expiry date. 2. Beware of scammers posing as personnel or partners to obtain personal information or money from you. We'll never ask you to do any financial transactions outside the app. To confirm information about your order, please check "My Orders" on your app or reach out to Support. 3. Beware of unsolicited calls asking you to turn off your device or to put your device on aeroplane mode while your order is being processed. Doing so might lead to the cancellation of your order. we will never request such things from you. 4. Verify if the email you received is from an official address. If you receive an email containing deals or promotions that seem suspicious to you, make sure to check if the sender’s email address looks legitimate. Official emails should always have the company’s official domain name . 5. Always keep your contact details updated on your account and turn on notifications for your app to receive important alerts from us. To update your details, head over to the "Profile" section on our app or web.

Sms verification not received

If you haven't received your sms verification code, check the number you entered is correct and that you have phone signal. If you still haven't received the text, please try requesting for the sms verification code again.
